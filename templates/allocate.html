About Us

This website improvises the working for M/s Prescience Calibration Ptv. Ltd. by maintaining a database for the workers and the Administrators.

By offering calibration service for torque transducers and angle encoder, power tools with transducer, and rotating torque tools, Prescience Calibration has earned a unique position among calibration laboratories. Prescience Calibration Pvt Ltd. is all compliance to ISO/ IEC 17025 (NABL Accreditation), the test and calibration laboratory quality standard. This implies a high level of technical competence and well documented, traceable calibrations. It is thus assured that the calibration services offered are in compliance with ISO 9000 and ISO/TS 16949 and any other standard as applicable. Laboratory also offers on-site calibration services. In addition to the accredited services, the laboratory offers simplified factory calibration of measuring equipment and verification of tools. The laboratory offers On-site calibration and process capability measurements or as called Measurement Capability Test are also offered. Calibration is the process of finding a relationship between two quantities that are unknown (when the measurable quantities are not given a particular value for the amount considered or found a standard for the quantity). When one of quantity is known, which is made or set with one device, another measurement is made as similar way as possible with the first device using a second device. The measurable quantities may differ in two devices which are equivalent. The device with the known or assigned correctness is called the standard. The second device is the unit under test, test instrument, or any of several other names for the device being calibrated.
